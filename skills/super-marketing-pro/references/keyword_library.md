### 长尾词 (Long-tail Keywords) - 低竞争高转化
这些词搜索量小，但搜索者的购买意图极强，是 SEO 的"黄金矿藏"：
- `waterproof PU leather for outdoor shoes`
- `GRS certified synthetic leather supplier`
- `automotive grade microfiber leather China`
- `custom color PU leather manufacturer`
- `PU leather vs genuine leather durability comparison`
- `how to source leather materials from China`
- `minimum order quantity PU leather China`

### 信息型关键词 (Informational Keywords) - TOFU 内容
这些词吸引处于学习阶段的潜在客户，建立品牌权威：
- `what is PU leather`
- `PU leather vs real leather`
- `how is synthetic leather made`
- `sustainable leather alternatives 2025`
- `best materials for sneakers`
